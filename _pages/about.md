---

## 🔍 Research Interests

My research focuses on building **LLM-based agent systems** capable of reasoning, memory integration, and adaptive learning.

🧠 **LLM Agents**  
I study how large language models can function as autonomous agents that interact with environments, perform long-horizon planning, and solve complex tasks through reasoning and tool use.

🗂 **Agentic Memory**  
I am interested in designing memory architectures that enable AI agents to store, retrieve, and refine experiences over time, supporting continual learning and more reliable long-term behavior.

📚 **Context Learning**  
I explore how intelligent systems dynamically construct and adapt context during reasoning, allowing models to generalize knowledge across tasks and environments.
