  <section class="home-section" aria-labelledby="research-overview-title">
    <div class="section-heading">
      <p class="section-label">Research Overview</p>
      <h2 id="research-overview-title">A systems view of intelligent decision-making</h2>
    </div>
    <p>
      My research aims to understand how language-model-based agents can move beyond short-horizon prompting toward
      more structured forms of deliberation and action. I am particularly motivated by the gap between strong
      in-context performance and the persistent challenges of planning, memory retention, and environment modeling in open-ended tasks.
    </p>
    <p>
      My current work focuses on how planning, memory, and world understanding can be combined into agentic systems
      that remain coherent, adaptive, and reliable across complex tasks.
    </p>
  </section>
